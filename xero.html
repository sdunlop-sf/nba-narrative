A Proposition for Xero · FY27

The signals are already there.
The engine isn't.

Xero knows more about its customers than almost any software company on the planet — usage, payments, sentiment, loyalty tier, product mix. The opportunity is connecting that intelligence to the right action, for the right customer, at the right moment — automatically, at scale.

The Challenge

Where the gap is
costing Xero growth

This isn't a data problem. Xero has the data. It's an execution problem — the gap between knowing what a customer needs and acting on it, at the moment it matters.

Challenge 01

Personalisation at scale remains a "Big Bet" — not a reality

The ambition to deliver always-on, 1:1 personalised experiences is explicitly on the FY27 roadmap. The tooling and team to make it happen are still being built. Every month without it, high-intent customers get a generic email.

Challenge 02

Partner Challenges are capped by manual overhead

FY26 delivered £1.2m ARR from 18 Challenges run globally. The FY27 target is £3m. The constraint isn't ambition — it's the engineering overhead and static segmentation that limit how many can run and how well they're targeted.

Challenge 03

Signals fire and expire without an action attached

A small business hits an account limit. A partner hasn't logged in for 30 days. A case resolves with five-star sentiment. Each one is a high-value moment — most pass unacted on because there's no system connecting signal to execution in real time.

Challenge 04

Propensity models exist in plan — not in production

The internal roadmap references feeding data into a centralised customer profile driving a purchase propensity model. Until that model is scoring and acting, the backbook is being managed by instinct, not intelligence.

The data exists. The platform exists. The intent is there. What's missing is the engine that connects signal to action — consistently, intelligently, for every customer in the base, not just the ones a team happened to prioritise this week.

The Solution

Six layers.
One connected system.

Each layer exists in isolation today. The value is in connecting them — so that a signal in Xero's data fires an intelligent decision, which delivers a personalised action, which learns from the response.

Layer 01
Data 360
Signal & Unification
The Signal
Unifies Xero's customer data — usage behaviour, product mix, payment patterns, support history, loyalty tier, firmographics — into a single live profile. Detects real-time events and segment changes the moment they happen.
"A Partner has added 3 new client organisations this month, is approaching the Gold tier threshold, and hasn't engaged with the latest Challenge."
Layer 02
Einstein Studio + Flow
NBA Engine
The Decision
Scores every customer against the active NBA catalogue. Runs propensity × business value arbitration across every eligible action. Selects the winning next best action — which offer, in which channel, with what message.
"Winner: Tier Progression Challenge — propensity 0.79 × business value 85 = highest ranked. Route via SFMC personalised email."
Layer 03
Promotions & Offers
Loyalty Management
The Content
The engine decides the action. Salesforce Promotions & Offers builds what the customer actually receives — the Challenge definition and its personalised expression per channel.
Promotion: "Add 2 new Xero organisations, earn 500 bonus points." Offer: "Hi Sarah — you're 2 orgs away from Gold. Here's a challenge built for where you are right now."
Layer 04
Salesforce Personalisation
Real-time ML
The In-Session Layer
Real-time, in-session ML that surfaces the right content when a customer or partner is actively in a Xero product or web property. Complements the outbound engine — the same intelligence, delivered in the moment of highest intent.
Partner logs into Xero Central → sees a personalised nudge about the Challenge they're close to completing. No new campaign required.
Layer 05
Agentforce Marketing
Agentic Execution
The Execution
Delivers the action across SFMC — email, SMS, in-app. Where two-way conversations are needed, an Agentforce agent handles replies, answers questions, and escalates to a human when the relationship demands it. Always-on. No campaign schedule required.
SB subscriber replies to a cross-sell email with a question → agent responds in real time, personalised to their product usage, and logs the outcome for model retraining.
Layer 06
Einstein Studio
Feedback & Learning
The Learning Loop
Every outcome — accepted, ignored, responded, converted — flows back as a training signal. Models retrain continuously. The engine gets smarter with every interaction. The team doesn't have to do anything differently for it to improve.
Challenge accepted → CampaignMember status updated → Data 360 ingests response → Einstein Studio retrains → propensity scores adjust for next run.
