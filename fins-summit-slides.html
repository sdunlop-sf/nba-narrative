The Memory Problem

Your AI is brilliant.
It just can't remember anything.

Most AI marketing tools reason well about a snapshot. But they start from scratch every time. They don't know what happened last week, last month, or last year.

What Forgetting Looks Like

Four scenarios your customers experience today.

The Rejected Offer

Customer declines a 20% discount. Three months later, the system offers the same discount again.

With memory: knows the offer was rejected, and the customer stayed anyway.

The Channel Contradiction

Marketing emails a credit card upsell Monday. Tuesday, the banker pitches the same card in-branch.

With memory: suppresses the card, surfaces the mortgage conversation instead.

The Slow Burn

Moderate spend, one product. A snapshot says: standard cross-sell. But spend is up 15% every month for five months.

With memory: sees trajectory, not just position. Recommends premium tier.

The Compliance Question

Regulator asks: why was this customer flagged? "The model scored them 0.82" is not an answer.

With memory: three signals, two thresholds, one policy rule. Full audit trail.
The Distinction That Matters

Channel memory vs Relationship memory.

  • Which variant won the A/B test?
  • What's the optimal send time?
  • Which campaign drove conversion?
