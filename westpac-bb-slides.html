The Art of the Possible · Sydney, 2028

Westpac Secures 25% SMB Market Share by Turning the 'Double You' into a Digital Co-Founder.

Two years ago, Westpac's Business Banking team identified a critical insight: business owners didn't want a flashier app — they wanted their financial admin to disappear. By integrating Salesforce's unified data engine and agentic architecture, Westpac transformed the iconic 'W' into a digital co-founder that predicts needs and automates solutions. Onboarding takes seconds, not days. Routine tasks require Zero UI.

I used to spend my Sunday nights sorting invoices and worrying about cash flow. Now Westpac's agent handles my supplier payments and flags potential cash crunches before they happen. My finances just work. It feels like they're actually on my side — not just holding my money. Trisha · Sydney-based florist
We stopped building processes that served the bank and started building agents that served the customer. We don't wait for a customer to apply for help anymore — if the data shows a logistics company is hitting a growth spike, we proactively structure the capital they need. We moved from doing to done.
