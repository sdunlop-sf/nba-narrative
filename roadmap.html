What is RTOM?

Real-Time Offer Management is Salesforce's AI-powered solution for offer creation, personalisation, deployment and analytics. It determines which offer — out of your full catalogue — is the right one for each individual customer, at each individual moment.

Where does it fit?

RTOM is the system of record for promotional offers. Data 360 provides the unified customer profile. RTOM selects the right offer from the catalogue and deploys it via Marketing Cloud. In a Mode 2 architecture, its offer catalogue becomes one input to the broader action engine.

RTOM also enriches Personalization's item graph — so the same offer surfaces consistently whether the customer receives an outbound campaign, visits the website, or opens the app. RTOM and Perso work hand in hand.
