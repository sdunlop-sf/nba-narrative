The Gap
The data exists across IAG.
There is no engine connecting it.
Policy systems, claims platforms, broker portals, and digital channels each hold a piece of the picture. Without a unified decisioning layer, that data never translates into the right action at the right moment.
Today — fragmented, reactive
With a unified decisioning layer
Policy systems and claims platforms are siloed. A customer's claim history, renewal date, and product holdings live in separate systems with no single view. Brokers and relationship managers work from incomplete information.
Data Cloud unifies every signal — policy holdings, claims history, broker interactions, digital behaviour — into a single real-time profile. Every decision is made with the full picture, not a fragment of it.
Broker engagement is relationship-led. Broker managers rely on personal knowledge, call schedules, and instinct to prioritise their portfolio. High-value broker relationships are under-served; at-risk ones are spotted too late.
NBA engine scores every broker relationship daily. Broker managers start the morning with a ranked list — who to call, what to discuss, which renewal is at risk, which cross-sell opportunity just opened. Data-led, not gut-led.
Customer engagement is campaign-driven. Marketing sends batch communications on schedules. Renewal outreach goes out weeks before expiry to entire segments — high volume, low relevance, reactive rather than predictive.
Real-time trigger-based engagement. Customer's renewal enters a Data Cloud segment 90 days out. Einstein scores the right offer. Marketing Cloud fires a personalised journey. The customer hears from IAG before they shop around.
No Next Best Action capability. There is no system that looks across policy data, claims history, digital signals, and broker activity and determines the single best action for a customer or broker relationship right now.
One decisioning brain governs all channels. Whether the output is a broker manager's daily brief, a customer app notification, or an agent-led conversation — the same engine, the same logic, the same measurable outcome.

Seven layers
One decisioning engine across broker, direct, and digital channels
01
Data Cloud
Unified Customer + Broker Profile
Ingests policy systems, claims history, broker portal activity, digital behaviour, and renewal data into a single real-time profile per customer and per broker. Every signal — a policy lapse, a claims spike, a broker going quiet, a digital renewal comparison — updates the profile the moment it fires. This is the foundation the decisioning engine reads at runtime.
02
Einstein Studio + Flow
NBA Scoring Engine
Trains on historical outcomes — which broker conversations converted, which renewal offers retained customers, which cross-sell signals led to policy upgrades. Scores each available NBA against the live profile and returns a ranked shortlist with probability scores. Flow orchestrates the eligibility → score → arbitrate → execute loop without requiring custom code.
03
Promotions & Offers
Policy & Product Offer Engine
Defines the commercial rules — eligible products, pricing bands, renewal incentives, broker commissions, campaign mechanics, and expiry limits. Every offer surfaced to a broker manager or customer is backed by a live, governed promotion — not a manually authored one-off with no audit trail. Consistent across NRMA, CGU, SGIO, and SGIC brands.
04
Financial Services Cloud
Broker & Relationship Manager Surface
The NBA brief surfaces inside the broker manager's existing CRM view — no new tool, no new login. Before calling a broker: the scored NBA, the rationale, the broker's portfolio trend, the renewal pipeline, the at-risk flags. The IIA relationship management team works from the same intelligence engine.
05
Marketing Cloud
Event-Driven Customer Journeys
Trigger-based renewal journeys, cross-sell nudges, and retention outreach — fired by real-time Data Cloud segments, not scheduled batch campaigns. Renewal date approaching + price comparison behaviour detected → personalised retention offer fires within minutes. Replaces the campaign calendar with event-driven intelligence across NRMA, CGU, WFI, and SGIO.
06
Agentforce
Digital Labour — Broker, Customer & Claims
The "Rollin" PoC extended into production-grade agentic capability. A broker agent surfaces renewal recommendations and portfolio gaps to broker managers autonomously. A customer agent handles renewal conversations, answers coverage queries, and escalates with full context. A claims agent triages, updates, and routes — reducing cost-to-serve without reducing the quality of the customer experience.
07
Feedback Loop
Model retrains on every outcome
Broker acts on an NBA → outcome logged → positive training signal → Einstein retrains. Customer accepts a renewal offer → signal captured → model improves on IAG's actual conversion patterns. After 3–4 months of live operation, the scores reflect IAG's real broker and customer behaviour — not generic insurance proxies. CBA's engine took years to reach this state; IAG can compress that timeline.
How It Works
From signal to action
in milliseconds — not days.
Every NBA follows the same five-step loop. It runs at runtime, for every customer and every broker relationship, continuously.
Step 01
Signal fires
Renewal date threshold, broker inactivity, claim completed, digital price comparison, policy lapse risk. Data Cloud segment membership updates in real time.
Step 02
Profile read
Full unified profile is fetched — policy holdings, claims history, broker relationship score, digital behaviour, suppression flags, consent status.
Step 03
NBAs scored
Einstein Studio runs propensity models against the available NBA catalogue. Each action gets an eligibility check and a probability score. The ranked shortlist is returned.
Step 04
Best action arbitrated
Business rules apply — frequency caps, offer eligibility, channel preference, commercial priority. The winning NBA is selected. One decision. Governed.
Step 05
Executed + fed back
Broker brief created in FSC. Customer email triggered in Marketing Cloud. Agent takes action autonomously. Outcome is logged → Einstein retrains.
Broker Channel
Broker manager's morning brief
Ranked broker portfolio by NBA score. Top priority: renewal at risk. Second: cross-sell opportunity just opened. Talking points and linked offer pre-loaded in FSC before the first call.
Direct Channel
Customer renewal journey
90 days before renewal, Data Cloud segment fires. Einstein scores retention vs cross-sell vs upgrade NBA. Marketing Cloud sends a personalised offer. Agentforce handles the conversation if the customer responds digitally.
Agentic Layer
Agentforce takes action autonomously
Claim submitted. Agentforce triages, updates the record, notifies the customer, flags anomalies, and routes complex cases — without a human in the loop. Every action is logged with full audit trail.
