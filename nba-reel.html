Salesforce.
The actual products powering each step.
The signal layer
Step 1 — Trigger
Data Cloud
Unifies every customer signal in real time. The moment a customer enters a segment — 60 days no order, cart abandoned, health score dropped — a Data Action fires the flow. No batch. No wait.
The brain
Step 2 — Score
Einstein Studio
A custom ML model trained on your data. Scores every active NBA for this customer — propensity, value, timing. The scores flow directly back into Salesforce Flow.
The orchestrator
Step 3 — Decide
Salesforce Flow
Loops through every candidate NBA. Checks eligibility, fatigue rules, and business gates. Arbitrates the winner. Then routes it — to a rep, a channel, or an agent.
The delivery
Step 4 — Execute
Agentforce
Surfaces the winning action — a rep task in Sales Cloud, a personalised email via Marketing Cloud, a portal offer, or a fully autonomous agent conversation. Every channel. One decision engine.
