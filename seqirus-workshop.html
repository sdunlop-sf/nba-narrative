The Goal

What does great look like
for Dr Clarke?

Right now
  • ✕Rep calls about flu vaccine — unaware she attended last week's symposium
  • ✕Email campaign goes to all GPs — same message, same timing, no context
  • ✕HCP portal shows generic content — not relevant to her elderly patient cohort
  • ✕No one at Seqirus has the full picture of her relationship
→
With Keystone
  • ✓Rep is briefed on her symposium attendance — conversation builds on it
  • ✓Email arrives at the right moment, with content matched to her specialty
  • ✓Portal surfaces high-dose clinical data — relevant to her over-65 patients
  • ✓Every team shares one view of Dr Clarke — field, digital, medical
Discussion: Is this the right vision? What's missing?

Alignment

Five principles.
Do we agree?

These are the guardrails we'll test every design decision against.

01
Consistency
Same concept, same structure — regardless of source system.
02
Scalability
New channels and use cases without redesigning core entities.
03
Activation-Ready
Every entity has a downstream use case. Data for its own sake is out of scope.
04
Privacy First
Consent is a first-class field. TGA and Privacy Act compliance designed in.
05
Future-Proof
Supports AI-driven use cases in Phase 2 without structural rework.
Which are uncontested? Which need discussion? Note caveats below.
