Mode 1 · ML at Scale
Einstein Studio + Salesforce Flow
A trained ML model scores every candidate action. Flow applies eligibility rules and arbitrates the winner — fast, deterministic, and auditable across millions of customers.
Mode 2 · Agentic Reasoning
Agentforce
An AI agent evaluates candidates using live context and reasoning — handling edge cases and nuanced situations that rules alone can't resolve. The two modes work in tandem.
Slack & Agentforce in the flow of work — bringing decisioning to where teams operate, optimising outcomes without switching context.
What It Delivers
From weeks to seconds
Campaign time-to-market drops from 6 weeks to sub-second. The trigger determines timing — not the campaign calendar.
Full coverage, not just priority accounts
The engine works across the entire customer base simultaneously. No account falls through the cracks because a rep didn't have bandwidth.
Digital labour absorbs the volume
Agents handle qualification and outreach at scale. Human reps focus on the conversations that actually require them.
A model that compounds with use
Every accept, decline, and ignore trains the model. After 3–4 months it reflects the customer's actual behaviour — proprietary, defensible, and impossible to replicate out of the box.
