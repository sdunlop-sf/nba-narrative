The Situation

CAR Group sends approximately 250 million transactional emails annually — enquiry confirmations, dealer responses, listing alerts. These arrive at the exact moment a buyer is most engaged: high intent, active in the funnel, ready to act.

Today, they deliver no intelligence. No recommendations. No next best action. No reporting. No ownership. They confirm. Nothing more.

The data foundation is done. Snowflake is implemented. Years of buyer behaviour, inventory signals, and engagement history are clean and primed. The infrastructure is ready. The activation layer is missing.
The Opportunity

The highest-ROI move available to CAR Group right now is not a new campaign or a new channel. It's turning 250 million transactional emails into 250 million intelligent decisioning moments.

An enquiry email sent to a buyer who has searched SUVs in a specific price range, visited three listings, and engaged twice in the last seven days is not a generic confirmation. It's an opportunity — to surface the next most relevant listing, offer a finance prompt, or trigger the right dealer follow-up — determined by a decisioning engine that understands this customer, this moment, and this context.

Done at scale, the impact compounds. Every click, conversion, and bounce becomes a training signal. The model learns. The next recommendation is better than the last.

The Vision

CAR Group becomes a business where every customer touchpoint — from the first enquiry email to the next app notification — is determined by intelligence, not a template.

The decisioning layer runs across web, app, and comms, unified by a single customer profile. Marketing owns the logic. Engineering doesn't own every change. Updates take hours, not months. And every action is auditable — you can explain why that recommendation was made to that customer at that moment.
