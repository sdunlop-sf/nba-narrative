Context · Compliance
07 / 15

The compliance questions
nobody can answer.

Data Residency

Which model touched which customer data? Which country did inference happen in? Can you prove EU data stayed in the EU?

Model Versioning

GPT-4o silently changes behaviour. Your outputs shift. Your compliance audit trail has no record it happened.

Prompt Audit Trail

When did the prompt change? Who approved it? Can you reproduce a decision output from six months ago exactly?

Right to Erasure

Customer invokes GDPR Article 17. Can you prove their data wasn't used to train a third-party model — in writing?

⚠️

In a vibe-coded stack, the honest answer to all four is: "We don't know." That is not an answer your legal team will accept.

What happens when
10 teams do this?

Content team builds a content engine. Service team builds a support bot. Marketing builds a lead scorer. Each one — a separate AI account, data store, and billing relationship.

No shared data layer

Each build has its own customer view. No single source of truth. Contradictory outputs in the same interaction.

No cost visibility

10 teams × 3 AI vendors = 30 separate invoices. Finance can't see the total. Nobody can govern it.

No institutional memory

Each build lives in the head of the person who made it. When they leave, so does the context.

What governed AI actually
looks like at scale.

Next Best Action decisioning — the enterprise equivalent of BGG's lead nurture AI, but governed, audited, and running across millions of customers.

Data Cloud
Unified customer profile — behavioural signals, transactions, engagement history. Single source of truth.
›
Einstein Studio
Binary classification model. Scores churn risk, financial distress, NBA propensity — per customer, in real time.
›
Agentforce · ARIA
Reads signals. Detects conflicts. Applies compliance rules. Adjudicates and selects a single empathetic action.
›
CRM Output
Campaign record created. Message drafted. Sent via right channel. Outcome retrains the model.
Mode 1 — Flow (~85%)

High-confidence signals. Einstein Studio score clears the threshold. Salesforce Flow executes automatically. Fast, deterministic, scalable.

Mode 2 — Agentforce (~15%)

Conflicting signals. Edge cases. Agentforce reasons through the tension, picks the safest action, and writes a rationale — audit-ready.

Adaptive Loop

Every customer response flows back to Data Cloud → Einstein Studio. The model improves with every accepted or rejected NBA.
