The Connected Stack
Built on infrastructure
Westpac already owns
No new vendors. No greenfield build. Seven layers that activate the investment already being made in Salesforce, Data Cloud, and Adobe.
Seven layers
One decisioning engine across institutional, business and consumer banking
01
Data Cloud
Unified Customer Profile
Ingests CRM, transaction history, Adobe AEP behavioural data, and product holdings into a single real-time profile. Every signal — a missed payment, a balance threshold, a mortgage enquiry — updates the profile the moment it happens. This is what the decisioning engine reads at runtime.
02
Einstein Studio + Flow
NBA Scoring Engine
Trains on historical CRM outcomes — which offers converted, which calls progressed. Scores each available NBA against the live customer profile and returns a ranked shortlist with probability scores. Flow orchestrates the eligibility → score → arbitrate → execute loop.
03
Promotions & Offers
Offer Eligibility Engine
Defines the commercial rules — eligibility criteria, offer mechanics, rate thresholds, expiry, redemption limits. The offer surfaced to a banker or customer is always backed by a live, governed promotion — not a manually authored one-off outside any audit trail.
04
Financial Services Cloud
Banker CRM Surface
The NBA brief surfaces inside the banker's existing CRM view — no new tool, no new login. Before a client call: the scored NBA, the rationale, the talking points, the linked offer. Institutional relationship managers and business bankers work from the same engine.
05
Salesforce Personalisation
Digital Channel Surface
Renders NBA decisions in digital touchpoints — the Westpac One app, internet banking, business portal. Real-time recommendations triggered by live Data Cloud segments. Complements Atomic.io already deployed — adds intelligence to the trigger logic.
06
Marketing Cloud
Journey Execution
Trigger-based email and SMS journeys fired by NBA signals. Mortgage renewal approaching → personalised rate offer. Business loan underutilised → targeted prompt. Cross-sell signal detected → banker notification + customer email in parallel. Event-driven, not batch.
07
Feedback Loop
Model retrains on every outcome
Banker acts on an NBA → outcome logged → positive training signal → Einstein retrains. Customer accepts an offer → signal captured → model improves. After 3–4 months of live operation, scores reflect Westpac's actual conversion patterns — not historical proxies.
