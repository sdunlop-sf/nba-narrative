The Connected Stack
Seven layers. One decisioning engine.
01
Data Cloud
Unified Customer Profile

Ingests CRM data, transaction history, Adobe AEP behavioural data, and product holdings into a single real-time customer profile. Every signal — a missed payment, a balance threshold crossed, a mortgage inquiry, a product click — updates the profile the moment it happens. This is what the decisioning engine reads at runtime.

02
Einstein Studio + Flow
NBA Scoring Engine

Trains on historical CRM outcomes — which offers converted, which calls progressed, which customers churned. Scores each available NBA against the live customer profile and returns a ranked shortlist with probability scores. Flow orchestrates the full eligibility → score → arbitrate → execute loop without engineering involvement once configured.

03
Promotions & Offers
Offer Eligibility Engine

Promotions define the commercial rules — eligibility criteria, offer mechanics, rate thresholds, expiry, and redemption limits. When the NBA engine fires, it checks which promotions the customer qualifies for. The offer surfaced to the banker or the customer is always backed by a live, governed promotion — not a manually authored one-off that sits outside any audit trail.

04
Financial Services Cloud
Banker CRM Surface

The NBA brief surfaces inside the banker's existing CRM view — no new tool, no new login, no change management friction. Before a client call, the banker sees: the scored NBA, the rationale, the talking points, and the linked offer. Institutional relationship managers and business bankers work from the same engine with role-appropriate views.

05
Salesforce Personalisation
Digital Channel Surface

Renders NBA decisions in digital touchpoints — the Westpac One app, internet banking, and the business banking portal. Real-time product recommendations and contextual prompts triggered by live Data Cloud segments. Complements the Atomic.io in-app notification layer already deployed — adds intelligence to the trigger logic.

06
Marketing Cloud
Journey Execution

Trigger-based email and SMS journeys fired by NBA signals from Data Cloud. Mortgage renewal approaching → personalised rate offer. Business loan facility underutilised → targeted product prompt. Cross-sell signal detected → rep notification + customer email in parallel. Replaces scheduled batch campaigns with event-driven execution tied to real customer signals.

07
Feedback Loop
Every action retrains the model

Banker acts on an NBA → outcome logged → positive training signal → Einstein retrains. Customer accepts an offer → signal captured → model improves. The engine gets progressively more accurate with every interaction across every channel. After 3–4 months of live operation the scores reflect Westpac's actual conversion patterns, not just historical proxies.
